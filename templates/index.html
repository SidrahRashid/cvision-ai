About CVision

Smarter Resume Intelligence

CVision is an AI-powered Resume Intelligence platform designed to help students, fresh graduates, and professionals create resumes that perform better in Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Powered by Google Gemini, CVision evaluates resumes, calculates ATS compatibility, identifies missing keywords, recommends improvements, suggests suitable career paths, generates interview questions, and produces a comprehensive professional report in seconds.
